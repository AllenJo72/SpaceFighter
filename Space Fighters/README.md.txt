>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>HOW TO PLAY<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<

1) Arrow Keys to move left and right.
2) SpaceBar to shoot.

>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>HOW TO WIN<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<

There is no part where you completely finish the game. Its more like getting the highest score.

>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>IN WHAT WAY YOU WILL LOSE<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<

Once the enemies reach a certain point near your character the game is over.


:::::::::::::::::::CLOSE THE GAME AND RESTART IF YOU WANT TO PLAY AGAIN ONCE YOU LOSE:::::::::::::::::::::::::
